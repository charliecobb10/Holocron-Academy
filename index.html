The Way

A padawan's path
through the models.

Welcome, young padawan. Seeking knowledge of the financial arts, you are. Hmm. Trained you shall be — not in the ways of the Force, but in the ways of the model. Patience, you must have. Disciplined, your spreadsheet must become. — Master Yoda

Holocron Academy follows the Stanford Graduate School of Business financial modeling curriculum. Each trial walks you through a real company, the way a real analyst would build it: assumptions at the top, clean formulas throughout, no hardcoded numbers buried in cells where they shouldn't be.

After you read the codex, you'll face a ten-question trial of knowledge. Pass it, and the case opens. You build the model in Excel, upload it, and a grader reviews your work cell by cell — with personalized feedback from Master Yoda.
