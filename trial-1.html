The Case — Netflix, 2005

You are an analyst at a sell-side equity research shop in early 2006. Your job: build a five-year operating model and cash flow projection for Netflix, from 2006E through 2010E.

The case provides historicals, market penetration assumptions, and three scenarios (Aggressive, Base, Conservative). The template gives you the skeleton — labels, headers, and the hardcoded driver inputs. Your task is to build every formula that connects them.
